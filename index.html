About me:

I was born in Canada during 1980. When I was young I got quickly addicted to computers. Life, however, took it's own turn and I didn't get anywhere into the computer market that I had planned when I was younger. For the past 20+ years I've been working as a cook but as COVID-19 recently hit the world over, I decided to take control of myself and used the time off work to learn a new skill and start working in the field I was always interested in but never had the resources to achieve.

I've also always wanted to work for myself, and with the way the world is these days, working remotely from home seems like a good idea that will allow me to achieve a steady finacial flow, keep from spreading COVID-19, and give back to a community and technology I've loved for so long.

In September 2020, I enrolled myself into a few courses on the Udemy learning platform, one of which was Web Developer. I've also taken extra courses in JavaScript, NodeJS, and MongoDB. I also plan on taking courses in Python and Java, and if my cards play right I'd like to get into Mobile Development.
